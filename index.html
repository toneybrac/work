About Us

We Are
Your Best Choice

Crows Group Limited CGL is a Holding Company based in Monrovia, with subsidiaries Crows Security, Crows Clearing and Forwarding, Crows Technology and crows Educational Consultantcy in partnership with Bay Alantic University USA.CGL has expertise in all of the above mentioned subsidiaries.

The corporation provides portfolios of services in business, professional development and international logistics management. CGL continues to serve the business community by providing solutions for critical business problems ranging from revenue maximization to cost management and also to technological problems while striving to meet the growing need for socially responsible and ethical business consultive services.
